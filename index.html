Diversity & Inclusion

A message from our CEO

As a collection of #NotNormal folks, we are truly phenomenal. As such, we are constantly looking at all corners around the globe for top talent waiting to be discovered. We never shy away from anyone but rather embrace their uniqueness. We see past race, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity, age, marital status, veteran status, disability, or any other factors as they add colour to our diverse team.

-Mahe Bayireddi

Our commitment to diversity runs deep. We believe diversity is essential to building a phenomenal workplace, product and customer experience. As we take collective action to build a more inclusive environment where all employees and candidates feel welcomed, and where differences in ideas and opinions are celebrated, we recognize there is more to be done. Our team is committed to advance until these powerful ideas are ingrained into our culture for you, for Phenom and for employers everywhere.

Employee Resource Groups at Phenom

It's important for all organizations to consider how to involve and embrace all people equally. That's why diversity and inclusion is not just something that we work towards for our own company, but tools that we have built into our product to help other organizations realize their full talent potential by creating Employee Resource Groups or ERGs.

Building a more inclusive workplace

At Phenom, our purpose is to help one billion people find the right job, but we realize the playing field isn't equal for everyone. The Phenom Inclusion, Equity and Diversity Initiative was designed to help tackle these challenges by driving critical conversations and taking action to build a more inclusive workforce for you, Phenom and employers everywhere.

International Women's Day

Not only are we focused on advancing diversity in our workplace, we love to celebrate it. In our India office, International Women's Day is a time to honor and celebrate our female employees.
